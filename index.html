The British Museum

The Rosetta stone is the Museum's most popular exhibit.The Rosetta Stone bears the priestly decree concerning Ptolemy V in three blocks of text in three different languages.The Parthenon Marbles,Sutton Hoo mask,ship burial collection and the Egyptian mummies are the must see exhibits in the museum.

Routemap to the British Museum from the LCY Airport:

The closest airport to the British Museum is the London City Airport.

  1. Head northwest on Hartmann Rd toward Camel Rd
  2. Take A1020, Lower Lea Crossing, Aspen Way/A1261, A1203 to Bloomsbury PI in Holborn
    1. From Came1 Rd take a right and you will find the route to A1020 on to your left
    2. From the Lower Lea Crossing take A1203 to reach Bloomsbury PI
  3. Drive to Great Russell St and then your destination will be on your right
